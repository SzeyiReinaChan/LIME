Prince of Peace Lutheran Church

Picture
    
All are welcome at Prince of Peace Lutheran Church. 
We’d love to have you join us for church at Prince of Peace this Sunday. Our
church is wheelchair accessible. Holy Communion Worship every Sunday morning at
9:30 a.m. Nursery service is available for young children during service.
Sunday School at 10:45 a.m. 

          Prince of Peace is a community of faith gathered in Christ’s name
to: 
                        Proclaim the Good News (Matthew 28: 19-20) 
                        Love God and each other (Matthew 22: 37-39) 
                        Be ministers of reconciliation (2nd Corinthians 5:
17-20) 
                        Practice hospitality and seek justice (Micah 6: 6)
