OUR MISSION
We, the people of Saint Mary’s Parish of Iowa City, are a Eucharistic community
whose mission is to 
worship and pray, to grow in faith and discipleship, and to serve those who are
in need.

We respect the traditions of the past 
and are open to the guidance of the Spirit as we move into the future.
