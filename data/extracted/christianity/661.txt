Messy Church

Next Messy Church - date and time to be confirmed, usuallly last Saturday of
the month

Children must be accompanied by an adult.

What is it? - Craft based church, and a meal, for all the family.

How does it work?

  • Time to chat and play board games over a coffee / tea / juice
  • Time to enjoy being together and making things, as a family
  • Short time of worship to celebrate and share what we have done together
  • Time to eat together

How much does it cost? - just some of your time
How do I book? - no need, just turn up!

A Word in Time - Methodist Online Bible Study


An online Bible study called A Word in Time. It features a daily Bible reading,
background and an explanation of the text, reflections and questions to ponder
from that week s contributor, and follows the readings in the Methodist Prayer
Handbook. You can read the study, or download audio files for each day s Bible
study to a computer or mp3 player and listen at a time convenient to yourself.



The Sanctuary Centre


'Towards the place where world and worship meet' - resources for outward
focused worship, including lots of prayers about wider world issues and
situations.



3 minute retreats


Changed daily, an opportunity to take a brief time out to relax, and reflect
and pray with a Bible passage. 'Three minutes a day can give you 24 hours of
peace'. Rooted in the Roman Catholic tradition.



Operation Noah - the churches' climate change campaign


Make a splash for climate justice with the churches' climate change campaign!
Sign the Climate Covenant.
