Mission Statement

 

The Mission of the Otterbein United Methodist Church is to know Christ and make
Christ known to all.

 

Philosophy of Ministry

 

 

 As a body of believers in Jesus Christ, it is our desire to welcome all
seekers and proclaim the good news of Jesus

to our neighbors, both near and far,

to the lonely,

to the grieving,

to the widows and widowers,

and to the children,

that they might be comforted by the Holy Spirit as revealed in God’s Holy Word.
