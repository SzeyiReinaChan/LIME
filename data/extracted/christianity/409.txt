DISCIPLESHIP SURVEY

Are you the Disciple you think you are? Are you the Disciple that God expects
you to be? Click anywhere on this message to sign up for a Discipleship Survey
sponsored by Rio Texas Conference of the United Methodist Church. The Survey
takes from 5 to 15 minutes and will give you a clear picture of where you are
in your growth to being a Disciple of Jesus Christ.  St. Matthew's UMC members
and visitors, please select "Las Misiones District" under the first drop-down
and "SAnt: St. Matthew's" under the second drop-down. After you sign up, a
login screen will open; log in to start your survey.
