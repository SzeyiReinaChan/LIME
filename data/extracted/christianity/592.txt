Who We Are

Who We Are

Our Mission: We exist to glorify God and enjoy a growing personal relationship
with Him as we:

  • Experience His grace.
  • Learn His truth.
  • Share His love with each other and the world.

 

What to Expect

What to Expect
CCC is an evangelical, Bible believing church. We are a non-denominational
church with members coming from a cross-section of church backgrounds. Our
common bond is a shared love for Christ, a commitment to the Word of God, and
our desire to tell others of God’s love and grace.
