St Mark's Methodist Church

Welcome to the St Mark's website. Whether you're a church member looking for
information, someone wanting to find out more, or have simply stumbled across
us, we hope you find something of interest and of use to you.

If you want to know how to find us, see our directions page. To find out how to
contact us, see our contacts page. This site also has information on church
services and what's on at St Mark's.

St Mark's is part of the Gloucestershire Circuit (see map) of the Methodist
Church of Great Britain.

On Saturday 17th November 2012 we re-opened our refurbished premises.
