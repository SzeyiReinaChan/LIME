Pastor's Message

From Fr. Dennis’ Desk BREAD AND PEACE Today is the Solemnity of the Epiphany
the Sunday when we celebrate the Manifestation of the Lord (Epiphania) as Light
of the world. Following the star, we recognize the true Light – Jesus. We also
inherit a challenge: Child of Bethlehem—house of bread; Man of Jerusalem – city
of peace; you have loved us without limit or condition; in our greatness and in
our misery, in our folly and in our virtue; may your hand be always upon us and
may your heart be within us so that we too may become bread and peace for one
another. [John Hammond, OSB] In what ways are we being called to be bread and
peace to others in the New Year...
