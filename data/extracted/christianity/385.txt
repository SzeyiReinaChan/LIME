Chuck Smith

 
Pastor Chuck Smith is best known for his expository style of preaching, as he
unpacked the truths of Scripture while working his way through the entire Bible
from Genesis to Revelation. These teachings are still being broadcast daily
around the world on his radio program The Word for Today. He founded and
pastored Calvary Chapel Costa Mesa, California, in the late 1960’s and
faithfully served there until October 3, 2013, when he entered into the eternal
presence of our Lord.
We also have added over 300 audio commentaries of Pastor Smith's, which are
available from the Audio/Video Menu.
