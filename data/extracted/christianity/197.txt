Mass Schedule

 

Weekends

  • Saturday vigil: 5:00 pm
  • Sunday: 8:00 am, 10:30 am & 7:30 pm

Weekdays

  • Monday-Friday: 7:45 am  in St. James Chapel
  • Monday-Friday:12:10 pm in the Cathedral

The Rosary is prayed daily at 11:40 am in the Cathedral.

 

 

Mass at Kingston General Hospital

Mass is celebrated at 9:30 am every Sunday for patients and their families at
Kingston General Hospital. The Chapel is located on Kidd 2. If you would like
to attend the Mass, please let your nurse know and a Volunteer will be happy to
escort you.

Sacrament of Reconciliation

  • 11:30 am to 12:00 noon Monday to Friday
  • Every Wednesday 1:00 pm to 5:00 pm
  • Saturdays 4:00 to 4:45 pm; other times by appointment
  • Sundays 7:20 am-7:50 am, 9:50 am-10:20 am, 7:00 pm to 7:20 pm
