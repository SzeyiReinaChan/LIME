Our Mission

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

We are a Spirit driven church family that nurtures faith in Jesus Christ
through worship, study, prayer, praise, fellowship, and service.

  •  Sharing the goodness of Jesus Christ.
  • Growing in Faith and Obedience to God.
  • Reaching out to our larger family through outreach ministries.
  • Creating a joyful music program.
  • Nurturing young people in the faith.
