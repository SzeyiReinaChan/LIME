Welcome

Our congregation in Swansea, South Wales consists of all age groups, with a
contemporary approach to both worship and community activities. We are a group
of people from all walks of life who enjoy a personal relationship with Jesus
Christ. Our Sunday services start at 10:30am and 6pm.
