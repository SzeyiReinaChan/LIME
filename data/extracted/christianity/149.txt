Welcome

Welcome to the Packs Branch Baptist Church web site. We have been online since
May of 2005. We invite you to look around the web site and enjoy learning about
our church. Here you can learn how to be saved, read our doctrinal statement,
read the weekly announcements and prayer list. You can find information on how
our church was founded, our pastor, our choir. We have an extensive archive of
audio messages. We have several hundred photos from the many activities. There
are several things to download for free, and much more! If you have any
questions or comments, please just send us an email.

We are an independent, fundamental, King James Bible, Missionary Minded church.
We would love for you to visit our church. If you do not have a home church we
would love for you to make ours YOUR home.

January 8th, 2012 - In the 7 years that our website has been online, this page
has been view over 27,000 times. That's not counting other pages at this
website that have been hit by bypassing the main index. If, out of those 27,000
hits, this website has made a difference to just one person, it has been well
worth the time and effort that it takes to maintain this website.

An Important Message For You

You need to know that everyone is born a sinner.

  • "Behold, I was shapen in iniquity; and in sin did my mother conceive me."
    --Psalm 51:5
  • "For all have sinned and come short of the glory of God." --Romans 3:23
  • "As it is written, there is none righteous, no not one." --Romans 3:10
  • "For there is not a just man upon earth, that doeth good, and sinned not."
    --Ecclesiastes 7:20

You need to know that man cannot save himself.

  • "For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: It is
    the gift of God." --Ephesians 2:8
  • "There is a way which seemeth right unto a man, but the end thereof are the
    ways of death." --Proverbs 14:12
  • "Not by works of righteousness which we have done, but according to His
    mercy He saved us, by the washing of regeneration, and renewing of the Holy
    Ghost. --Titus 3:5

You need to know that the blood of Christ is the only thing that can wash away
sin.

  • "How much more shall the BLOOD of Christ, who through the eternal Spirit
    offered Himself without spot to God, purge your conscience from dead works
    to serve the living God? --Hebrews 9:14
  • "But if we walk in the light as He is in the light, we have fellowship one
    with another, and the BLOOD of Jesus Christ His Son cleanseth us from all
    sin. --1 John 1:7

You need to know that you must believe, repent, and receive the Lord into your
heart to be saved.

  • "I tell you, Nay: but except ye repent, ye shall all likewise perish."
    --Luke 13:3
  • "That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt
    believe in thine heart that God hath raised Him from the dead, thou shalt
    be saved. --Romans 10:9
  • For WHOSOEVER shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved." --
    Romans 10:13
