Welcome to Little Chapel United Methodist Church

"The Church Gathered To Worship Scattered To Serve”

We are a historic Spirit filled community of faith that believes all are called
to mission and ministry by engaging in ministry with the poor, combating
diseases of poverty, and developing principled Christian leaders for the good
of all humankind. We lovingly invite all to come and experience dynamic
worship, community outreach, and loving fellowship.
