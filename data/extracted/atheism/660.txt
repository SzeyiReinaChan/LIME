Sen. John Cooke works to remove 10-year sex-assault statute of limitations

The Tribune, January 23, 2016

Greeley’s Sen. John Cooke, the former Weld County Sheriff, wants to remove the
statute of limitations on sexual assault crimes.

The Republican senator is working across the aisle with Rep. Rhonda Fields,
D-Aurora, to completely repeal Colorado’s 10-year statute of limitations on
felony sexual assault crimes, such as rape.
