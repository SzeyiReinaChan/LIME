  • ABOUT US

    MICHIGAN ATHEISTS IS A STATEWIDE, NONPROFIT, NONPARTISAN, SOCIAL AND
    EDUCATIONAL ORGANIZATION DEFENDING ATHEISTS CIVIL RIGHTS AND DEDICATED TO
    THE SEPARATION OF STATE AND CHURCH.

    MICHIGAN ATHEISTS WAS ORGANIZED IN 1975 AND HAS BEEN DEFENDING SEPARATION
    OF STATE AND CHURCH SINCE IT’S INCEPTION.  WE ARE THE OLDEST LOCAL ATHEIST
    GROUP IN THE COUNTRY.  IN 2011, MICHIGAN ATHEISTS WAS NAMED THE NUMBER ONE
    ATHEIST ORGANIZATION IN THE COUNTRY BY AMERICAN ATHEISTS INC.

    MICHIGAN ATHEISTS IS AN OPEN INVITATION ORGANIZATION, THERE IS NO
    APPLICATION PROCESS AND THERE ARE NO DUES.
