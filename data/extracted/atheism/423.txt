We are mostly members of the Humanist Association of Connecticut who dine
together and discuss humanist issues at least once a month. We welcome other
humanists to join us!

The Humanist Association of Connecticut is a member organization of the
Connecticut Coalition of Reason and a chapter of the American Humanist
Association.
