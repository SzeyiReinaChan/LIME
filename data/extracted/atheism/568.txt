NOVA Atheists is a meetup for the growing Atheist community in Northern
Virginia and suburban DC, focused on Atheism, Separation of Church and State,
the advancement of Science, social structure for the Atheist community, and
activism in pursuit of those goals.

NOVA Atheists the social meetup organization for Beltway Atheists, Inc. 

NOVA Atheists is an American Atheists affiliate.
