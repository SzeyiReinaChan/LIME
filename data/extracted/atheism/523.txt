We are Forsyth Area Critical Thinkers. We are a Non-Profit 501 (c)(3) dedicated
to the advancement of science, reason, and equality in North Carolina. We are
based out of Winston Salem and our group is diverse and open to all who
appreciate critical thinking and good conversation. Whether you are just
looking to find some like minded friends, or if you want to make a difference
in your area, F.A.C.T. has a place open for you. Among friends.
