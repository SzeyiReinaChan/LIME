Montgomery Area Freethought Association is a social group for atheists,
agnostics, skeptics, freethinkers, humanists, brights, and the non-religious.
We provide fellowship and compassion to those who feel alienated by their
thoughts and questions about their beliefs. Gather at our weekly meetings to
meet other local like-minded individuals and discuss ideas, philosophy, and
problems unique to unbelievers. Come to our monthly meetups  and participate in
interesting discussions.

Montgomery Area Freethought Association  is a member of the Secular Coalition
for Alabama, an American Atheists affiliate, and is Military Association of
Atheists and Freethinkers friendly.
