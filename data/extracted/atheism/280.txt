A group for atheists from Frankston, the Mornington Peninsula and other
southern suburbs of Melbourne (or those further away who are happy to commute).

Meet other local atheists & freethinkers for monthly meetups, conversation &
friendship! The format for our meetups is usually an informal round table
discussion on a chosen topic .

Contact: Greg Plier

email: peninsulaatheists@gmail.com

phone: 0402 355 508

    Atheism - the acceptance that there is no credible scientific or factually
    reliable evidence for the existence of a god, gods or the supernatural.
