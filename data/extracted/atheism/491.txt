A secular community of atheists and atheist friendly people in the Detroit
Area, including , but not limited to, agnostics, skeptics, freethinkers,
humanists, non-theists, anti-theists, untheists, brights, rationalists,
doubters, heathens, heretics, the curious, and their friends. The Detroit
Atheists' Meetup is a member of the Detroit Coalition of Reason, http://
DetroitCoR.org.
