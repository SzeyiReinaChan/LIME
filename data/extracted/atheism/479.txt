This is the place for atheists, freethinkers, humanists, deists (or whatever
secular label you go by) to come together for food, talk, and speakers. Seeking
a place where you can talk about atheism and bounce ideas off fellow
freethinkers? If so, this is the place for you.

Also see our website at http://www.louisvilleatheists.com
